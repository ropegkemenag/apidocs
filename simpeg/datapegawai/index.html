Get Filter Pegawai

POST /v1/pegawai HTTP/1.1
Host: https://api.kemenag.go.id
Content-Type: application/x-www-form-urlencoded
Authorization: Bearer eyJ0xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
Parameter
  • satker: Kode Unit Kerja Atasan
  • limit: Limit
  • start: Mulai Pencarian
